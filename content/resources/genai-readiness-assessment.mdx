# GenAI Readiness Assessment

**For:** Operations Leaders, IT Directors, SMB Owners, Strategy Teams
**Time to complete:** 5–8 minutes

---

Most organizations are asking the wrong first question about AI. Instead of "What AI tools should we buy?" the better question is: **"Are we ready for AI — and ready in the right ways?"**

This assessment helps you find out. Answer 20 questions across four critical dimensions of organizational AI readiness:

1. **Data Readiness** — the quality, availability, and governance of your data
2. **Talent & Culture** — your people's AI literacy and your culture's openness to change
3. **Governance & Risk** — your policies, oversight, and risk management for AI
4. **Use Case Maturity** — how well you've identified and prioritized AI opportunities

Your results will include a readiness tier (Exploring, Building, or Scaling) with a dimension-by-dimension breakdown and personalized action recommendations.

---

## Why This Matters

Organizations that rush into AI without assessing readiness often face the same problems:

- Tools get adopted without data to support them
- Employees use AI in unauthorized ways because there's no policy
- Pilots fail because success metrics weren't defined
- Leadership support evaporates when results aren't visible

Readiness isn't about being perfect before you start. It's about knowing where your gaps are so you can address them strategically as you move forward.
