Is This Right For You

For founders who are serious
about 10X outcomes.

Embedded is high commitment on both sides. If you recognize yourself here, you are ready.

  • You know that the limiting variable to your growth is not product or engineering, it is go-to-market and commercial infrastructure.

  • You have or plan to raise capital and want a seasoned operator at the table for every major decision before and after the capital arrives.

  • You recognize the value of a relationship advantage: partnerships, investor access, and strategic clarity that your competitors do not have.

  • You are 3 to 5 years from exit and know that the commercial infrastructure built today will determine your exit multiple in that timeframe.

  • You want a revenue partner who has skin in the game: invested in your success, accountable for results, and committed to the journey.
