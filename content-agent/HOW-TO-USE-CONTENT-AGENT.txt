LYNNFERNANDO.COM — CONTENT AGENT GUIDE
=======================================


WHAT IT DOES
------------
The content agent automatically writes articles in your voice and publishes
them to the Resources section of lynnfernando.com. It runs every Monday at
8:03am without you having to do anything — as long as your Mac is on.


HOW IT WORKS
------------
1. You add article topics to the queue file (articles-queue.txt)
2. Every Monday morning the agent picks the next topic, writes a 400–500 word
   article using Claude AI, and deploys it live to your website
3. The Resources hub page (lynnfernando.com/resources) updates automatically
4. Everything gets logged to publish.log so you can see what ran


FILES & WHAT THEY DO
--------------------
articles-queue.txt       Add new article topics here
articles-manifest.json   Master list of all published articles (auto-updated)
voice-guide.md           Lynn's writing style and tone rules fed to the AI
publish.sh               The script that runs automatically every Monday
agent.js                 The AI writing engine (powered by Claude API)
publish.log              Log of every run — check this if something looks wrong


ADDING ARTICLES TO THE QUEUE
-----------------------------
Open articles-queue.txt and add new topics at the bottom using this format:

    TOPIC: Your Article Title Here
    CATEGORY: Women in Business
    ANGLE: The specific argument or angle you want the article to take

    ---

Available categories:
  - Women in Business
  - Starting or Acquiring a Business
  - Philanthropy & Social Impact
  - GenAI & Business
  - Empire Building
  - Investing

ANGLE is optional but recommended — it gives the AI a clear direction and
makes the article sharper and more specific to your point of view.

Always separate articles with three dashes on their own line:  ---


AUTOMATIC SCHEDULE
------------------
Runs:    Every Monday at 8:03am
Requires: Your Mac to be on and awake at that time

If your Mac is asleep on Monday morning, the job will be skipped for that week.
It will NOT catch up — it simply waits until the following Monday.

Queue check: When only 2 articles remain in the queue, a warning appears in
publish.log. That's your signal to add more topics.


RUNNING IT MANUALLY
-------------------
To write and publish the next article right now (without waiting for Monday):

    cd "/Users/lynnfernan/Lynn Priorities/Personal Brand/content-agent"
    bash publish.sh

To write all remaining articles in the queue at once:

    cd "/Users/lynnfernan/Lynn Priorities/Personal Brand/content-agent"
    node agent.js --all
    bash ../deploy.sh lynnfernando.com 35.80.180.230 ~/Downloads/LightsailDefaultKey-us-west-2.pem

To regenerate the Resources hub page without writing a new article:

    cd "/Users/lynnfernan/Lynn Priorities/Personal Brand/content-agent"
    node agent.js --index
    bash ../deploy.sh lynnfernando.com 35.80.180.230 ~/Downloads/LightsailDefaultKey-us-west-2.pem


CHECKING WHAT RAN
-----------------
Open publish.log in any text editor. Each run shows:
  - Date and time it ran
  - How many articles were in the queue
  - Whether the article was written successfully
  - Whether the deploy succeeded
  - A warning if the queue is getting low


IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG
------------------------
Article failed to generate:
  → Check publish.log for the error message
  → Make sure your Mac has internet access
  → Your API key may have expired — generate a new one at console.anthropic.com
    and update it in ~/.zshrc (replace the ANTHROPIC_API_KEY line)

Deploy failed (article written but not live):
  → Make sure the file ~/Downloads/LightsailDefaultKey-us-west-2.pem exists
  → Run the deploy manually:
    bash "/Users/lynnfernan/Lynn Priorities/Personal Brand/deploy.sh" lynnfernando.com 35.80.180.230 ~/Downloads/LightsailDefaultKey-us-west-2.pem

Queue is empty:
  → Add new topics to articles-queue.txt and save — the next Monday run
    will pick them up automatically


API KEY
-------
Your Anthropic API key is stored in ~/.zshrc
If it ever stops working, generate a new one at: console.anthropic.com
Then open ~/.zshrc and replace the ANTHROPIC_API_KEY line with the new key.
Never paste your API key into a chat, email, or document.

WEEKLY MAINTENANCE (5 MINUTES)
-------------------------------
1. Check publish.log on Monday to confirm the article went live
2. Visit lynnfernando.com/resources to see it on the site
3. When the queue warning appears, spend 10 minutes adding new topics
   to articles-queue.txt — aim to keep at least 4 weeks ahead


QUEUE CAPACITY GUIDE
--------------------
  4 topics  = 1 month of content
  8 topics  = 2 months of content
  12 topics = 3 months of content (recommended minimum to maintain)
