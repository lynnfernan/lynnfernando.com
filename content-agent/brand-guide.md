## Voice Notes for Social

### LinkedIn
- Open with a hook — no "I" as the first word (hurts reach)
- Thought leadership tone — share the insight, not just the link
- 150–250 words for launch posts, 100–180 for insight posts
- End with article URL and 3–5 hashtags
- Line breaks matter — short punchy lines outperform dense paragraphs

### Instagram
- More personal, direct, and visual in tone
- "Link in bio" — never paste the URL in the caption
- 80–140 words in the caption body
- Hashtags at the end, separated by a line break
- 15–20 hashtags per post
- Tag @lynnfernandoofficial, @revglobalinc in every post
- Tag @ayanafoundation_ when relevant to philanthropy or impact topics
