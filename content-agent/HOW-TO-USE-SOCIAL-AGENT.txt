LYNNFERNANDO.COM — SOCIAL MEDIA AGENT GUIDE
============================================


WHAT IT DOES
------------
The social media agent reads each published article and generates 4 ready-to-use
social media posts — 2 for LinkedIn and 2 for Instagram. Posts are saved as .txt
files you can open and copy from. It runs automatically after every article publishes.


HOW IT WORKS
------------
1. An article publishes to your site (automatically every Monday)
2. The social agent reads the article content
3. Claude AI generates 4 posts tailored to the platform and category
4. Posts are saved to content-agent/social-posts/ as a dated .txt file
5. You open the file, review the posts, and copy-paste to LinkedIn and Instagram


WHAT YOU GET PER ARTICLE
-------------------------
LinkedIn Post 1 — Launch post (150–250 words)
  Opens with a strong hook. Thought leadership tone. Ends with the article
  URL and 3–5 relevant hashtags.

LinkedIn Post 2 — Insight angle (100–180 words)
  A different take on the same article — a specific idea, counterintuitive
  point, or insight. Not a repeat of Post 1.

Instagram Post 1 — Launch caption (80–140 words)
  Direct and personal tone. Ends with "Link in bio ↑" instead of a URL.
  Followed by 15–20 hashtags and account tags.

Instagram Post 2 — Pull quote (50–80 words)
  Built around one powerful line from the article. Short and punchy —
  designed to work as a standalone quote card post.

RUNNING IT MANUALLY
-------------------
To generate posts for the most recent article:

    cd "/Users/lynnfernan/Lynn Priorities/Personal Brand/content-agent"
    node social-agent.js

To generate posts for a specific article by slug:

    cd "/Users/lynnfernan/Lynn Priorities/Personal Brand/content-agent"
    node social-agent.js --slug article-slug-here

The slug is the filename of the article without .html
Example: why-women-led-businesses-win-when-markets-get-hard


AUTOMATIC SCHEDULE
------------------
The social agent runs automatically every Monday after the article publishes.
No action needed — just open the social-posts/ folder on Monday to find your posts.

Full Monday sequence:
  8:03am  → New article written and deployed to lynnfernando.com
           → resources/index.html updated
           → 4 social posts generated and saved to social-posts/


IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG
------------------------
Posts didn't generate:
  → Check publish.log for the error
  → Run manually from the content-agent folder (see above)
  → Make sure your API key is still valid at console.anthropic.com

MODULE_NOT_FOUND error:
  → You're running the script from the wrong folder
  → Always cd into content-agent/ first before running node social-agent.js

Posts feel off-brand:
  → Open content-agent/voice-guide.md and update the tone or style rules
  → Open content-agent/brand-guide.md to update hashtags or account handles
  → Re-run the agent — it will generate fresh posts using the updated guides
