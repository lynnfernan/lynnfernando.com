From Fortune 100
to Empire Builder.

Lynn Fernando is a Brand & Commercialization Architect with a track record of building, scaling, and transforming businesses across industries. Her career began inside Fortune 100 companies like Disney and Amazon, where she developed deep expertise in brand architecture, partnerships, growth, and commercialization strategy.

But she quickly realized she wasn't meant to operate within systems—she was meant to build them. Today, Lynn leads REV Global as CEO & Principal, alongside her work as Co-Founder of the Ayana Foundation.

She works with founders, investors, and institutions to architect their brands, commercialize their businesses, and unlock new growth through AI-powered systems.

At the same time, she is building her own portfolio across real estate, private equity, and alternative investments—designed to create long-term, generational wealth that compounds for decades.

Beyond business, Lynn is a dedicated mother of two boys, philanthropist, and advocate for women's economic empowerment. Through the Ayana Foundation, she is helping fund and mentor women founders globally—creating access where it has historically been limited. Lynn received her MBA and undergraduate degrees in Communications and Psychology from The University of Michigan.
