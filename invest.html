Capital & Investments · REV Capital

I deploy capital.
I don't take cold pitches.

Lynn deploys capital across two tracks: institutional structures through REV Capital — SPVs, Qualified Opportunity Zone Funds, and Oil & Gas direct participation — and a personal portfolio spanning angel investing, real estate, and private credit. Deals reach her through introductions, not inboxes.

Institutional · REV Capital

Structured Capital for Qualified Opportunities

Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs)

Co-investment vehicles and syndication structures for accredited investors entering high-conviction deals alongside REV Capital.

Qualified Opportunity Zone Funds (QOZFs)

Real estate development and operating businesses in federally designated Opportunity Zones. Tax-advantaged structures under IRC § 1400Z-2. Active across real estate and business QOZ investments.

Oil & Gas Direct Participation

Direct participation programs with intangible drilling cost (IDC) deductions for accredited investors. Qualified sponsors only — programs are reviewed by the REV Capital team before any capital conversation.

Personal Portfolio

Direct Investing Across Three Verticals

Angel Investing

Early-stage equity at pre-seed and seed. Focus on operators building in sectors where Lynn brings domain expertise — AI, healthcare, real estate technology, and commerce infrastructure.

Real Estate

Short-term rentals, mid-term rentals, ground-up development, and Opportunity Zone plays. Active across multiple markets with a long-term, cash-flow and appreciation focus.

Private Credit

Structured lending, bridge financing, and preferred equity instruments. Selective and relationship-driven.

What I Evaluate

Investment Criteria

These are the filters I apply before any capital conversation begins. If your deal clears them, there is a path forward. If it doesn't, I'd rather tell you now.

Deal Source
Warm introductions only. If we haven't been introduced through a mutual contact, this isn't the right path. REV Global's network spans private equity, real estate, and venture — most qualified sources already have a route in.
Sponsor / Operator Track Record
History, fund structure, and alignment are reviewed before the underlying asset. For institutional deals, REV Capital evaluates the sponsor before evaluating the opportunity.
Asset Class
Deals must fall within the six categories listed above: SPVs, QOZFs, Oil & Gas, angel equity, real estate, or private credit. Adjacent categories are not considered.
Stage (Angel)
Pre-seed and seed only. Series A and beyond are not in scope for personal angel allocation.
Geography
U.S.-domiciled structures by default. Opportunity Zone investments are U.S. only by statute.
Check Size
Not publicly disclosed. That conversation comes after qualification — not before.

If your deal clears these filters, there is a path forward. If it doesn't, I'd rather tell you now than let you invest time in the wrong direction.

Before you reach out

  • This page has no generic "pitch me" link. That's intentional.
  • Unsolicited decks sent to my contact address are not reviewed.
  • This page is not for advisors, consultants, or vendors seeking introductions to the REV Global network.
  • Oil & Gas and Opportunity Zone programs must come through qualified sponsors. Unsponsored deals are not considered.
  • If you're a founder pitching at a conference, on LinkedIn, or via cold email — the answer is no. The path is always through my network, and my network knows that.
How This Works

How Deals Reach Me

There is no cold path. There is a warm path — and it works.

Warm Introduction

A mutual contact makes an introduction. REV Global's network spans private equity, real estate, and venture. If you have a deal that fits the criteria, someone in that network already knows how to reach me.

Structured Intake

After an introduction is made, deal sources complete the form below. It is not a pitch — it is the prerequisite for a conversation. The form asks qualifying questions that take about five minutes to answer honestly.

Review

Institutional deals are reviewed by the REV Capital team. Personal investments are reviewed directly by Lynn. A substantive conversation happens before any capital discussion — and only after the intake clears.

Qualified Deal Sources Only

Submit a Deal for Review

This form is for deal sources who have been introduced to Lynn through her network. Completing it does not guarantee a response. All submissions are reviewed by the REV Capital team.
