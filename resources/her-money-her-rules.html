Her Money, Her Rules: The Financial Education They Never Gave Us

This is not a talk about saving more. It's a talk about building more.

Lynn Fernando · Keynote Speaker & Entrepreneur

The Talk

Most women were taught to manage a budget. Very few were taught to build wealth. That gap is intentional — and it's costing us. This keynote closes it. Lynn breaks down the investment vehicles, equity structures, passive income frameworks, and ownership mindsets that turn income earners into asset owners. Practical, accessible, and urgent — this is not a talk about saving more. It's a talk about building more. Because financial literacy isn't just smart. It's an act of self-respect, and it's the foundation of every empire.

What Audiences Take Away
  1. 1 The difference between financial management and wealth building — and why most women are only taught the first
  2. 2 The investment vehicles that create passive income: real estate, equity, private markets, and alternative assets
  3. 3 How to evaluate an investment opportunity — the framework Lynn uses herself
  4. 4 Why your network is your net worth — and how to access rooms where capital moves
  5. 5 The first three wealth-building moves to make in the next 90 days, regardless of where you're starting
