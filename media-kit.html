Lynn Fernando is a global entrepreneur, investor, and strategic advisor known for building and scaling businesses at the intersection of capital, technology, and growth.

Lynn Fernando is a global entrepreneur, investor, and empire builder redefining how wealth, business, and impact intersect.

After building her foundation inside Fortune 100 companies like Disney and Amazon, she transitioned into entrepreneurship, where she now leads REV Global—a multi-platform ecosystem spanning advisory, capital formation, and investor communities.

Lynn works with high-growth companies and investors to unlock scale through AI transformation, strategic growth, and capital deployment. Her investment portfolio spans private equity, real estate, defense manufacturing, and wellness, with a focus on high-leverage, tax-advantaged strategies.

She is also the Co-Founder of the Ayana Foundation, a global initiative dedicated to empowering women founders through capital, mentorship, and access.

As a speaker, Lynn is known for her bold, actionable insights on building wealth, scaling businesses, and stepping into leadership with clarity and conviction.
