The Institutional Difference

A fractional CMO can build a campaign. An architect builds the rooms.

Most operators at the lower middle market and PE-backed portco level don't need another marketing consultant or a fractional CMO running paid media. They need someone who has sat across from buyers at the largest companies in the world, structured the deals, and knows what an institutional commercial operation actually looks like.

The work is unglamorous and high-leverage: which channel partners actually move volume, which platform integrations create lock-in, which contract terms compound, which exec relationships unlock the next category. The asset isn't a deck. It's access to rooms most operators can't enter alone, paired with a playbook that has worked at Fortune 100 scale.

That's the work, and it's what makes a business acquirable at a premium multiple or defensible at the next stage of growth.
